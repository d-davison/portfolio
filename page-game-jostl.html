JOSTL. is a Sports-Tech company based in London. JOSTL. a fan engagement platform with a re-imagined match experience.

At JOSTL. I worked as a solo front-end developer, working collaboratively with a back-end developer, and a UI designer. Working on implementing many features, from the planning phrase into the final development.

My main responsibilities & accomplishments at JOSTL.

The JOSTL. Social Feed

The JOSTL. Social Feed was a large feature with the aim to bring the most relevant and newest content to the user, with a similar user experience to that of TikTok or Instagram Reels. The user would be able to flick through posts, and interact with a variety of content including match-stats, generated questions, and user generated content.

The main mechanics of the flicking were created, with an easy inspector view created for collaborative design sessions with the designer so we could easily decide on what settings we wanted going for the smoothest and most intuitive user experience.

After that, we worked on designing and then developing an initial 30 post types, complete with different interactions. After the initial 30 we have worked on designing and developing 26 new post types. With the easy and expandable system, it was easy to develop, implement, and deploy new post types.
