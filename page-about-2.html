About Me

Hi, I'm Dan. I'm a software developer, primarily with experience developing apps and games for mobile and VR

I have 4 years of industry experience where I have developed Virtual Reality Experiences, worked on eLearning Mobile Games, and developed Sports applications using real-time sports data. as well as this, I have worked freelance, developing client visions from the initial concept phase, to publishing and releasing apps on both Google Play Store and the Apple App Store.

  • Helped develop back-end and front-end development tools.
  • Worked collaboratively with developers and designers to create apps and games.
  • Worked directly with clients to fully captivate a client's vision
